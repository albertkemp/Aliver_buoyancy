Flight Simulator (About)

About our Flight Simulator

I made the flight simulator, by styling <div>s with CSS, for example adjusting the border radius, background color, border, and position on the page. So after that I came up with an airplane something like this:


An image of the airplane div, styled first.


Later, after about 1,000,000,000 bugs, I finally made a new model of the div, which I was all set for styling the rest of the simulator. The new div was longer and more like an airplane than the older one.

The second Airplane Div format


After that, I put in a title, so everything was in a format, with an Aliver Sims Flight Simulator title, and a speed indicator that is currently on 100%/0.12s, which means that it goes 100% across the page in 0.12 seconds. That is my current speed, and as I hadn't later adjusted it, it came out as that.

So I had everything currently how I wanted, and I started thinking about how to make it move. A moving background video was tried, but it kept appearing on top, and I didn't like it anyway. Then I came up with an idea about making something on the bottom move, which would give the illusion that the aircraft was moving. So I went to a simple W3schools page, and it showed code for a red square div moving from up to down 3 times at a speed of something much larger than I have now. So, with some adjustments to code, I turned Up, to Left, and Down, to Right. So I had a square moving from left to right. Then, I turned 3 to a really big number, something billion, and the current speed much faster, so now I had a rapidly looping square moving from right to left. Then, I just put in the basic javascript functions and CSS, and changed the square to a really long white rectangle, like dashed lines on a road. Then I put it on a black div so the airplane was flying over a runway. Video below:


The airplane flying over the runway.


So then, I got how the airplane flew over the runway, and finally with lots of JavaScript code I had got it working. Then it was a simple matter at home to make a new version with clouds going past and the airplane rolling across the runway!
